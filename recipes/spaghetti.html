Spaghetti

How to make spaghetti at home?

Making spaghetti can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 pound dried spaghetti
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Finely chop 1 medium yellow onion and mince 3 garlic cloves.
  2. Add ingredients to the pot.
  3. Add tomatoes, wait to boil.
  4. Add spaghetti.
  5. Wait and serve.
