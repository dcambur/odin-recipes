Coca-Cola

How to make coca-cola at home?

Making Coca-Cola can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Lemon Juice
  • Black paint (organic)
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to pot.
  2. Boil.
  3. Add a lot of sugar.
  4. Wait for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Cool down and serve.
